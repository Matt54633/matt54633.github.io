Established 2020

Founded in Yeovil, Somerset - Mobile Car Mechanic South West offers professional and reliable services across a 30 mile radius spanning Somerset, Dorset and Mendip. We are fully insured and have more than 30 years of experience.

We travel to your location to diagnose and repair your vehicle, saving you the time and hassle of going to a traditional garage. We are equipped with the latest tools and technology to handle a wide range of repair and maintenance services for any situation.

With our competitive prices and exceptional customer service, we are your go-to choice for all of your car care needs!
